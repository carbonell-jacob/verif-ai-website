Why It Matters

Knowledge Is
Your Best Defense

AI-generated media is being used to spread false narratives, manipulate public opinion, and deceive individuals. Understanding how it works — and what to look for — is the first and most powerful line of defense.

  • Protect yourself from deepfake scams & impersonation
  • Critically evaluate viral images and videos online
  • Understand the social and political risks of synthetic media
  • Share knowledge to build a more media-literate community
