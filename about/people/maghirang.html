About Chris Ivan Maghirang

Hi, I’m Chris Ivan Maghirang, a second-year Computer Science student at the University of the East Manila. The reason I chose this field is because I had electives befoore during my highschool days that is connected with programming. Although I am familiar, I had real struggles up until now in learning different kinds of tech-related projects. But this doesn't stop me from growing, learning, and adapting whether as a person or programmer. For me, as long a I persevere and continue what I am doing, I can achieve what I want in life.

Aside from studies, I am really a fan of playing volleyball, it's my hobby actually. It's quite the opposite of having this program since CS uses technology and logic, while my hobby doesn't. It's actually my dream to be a varsity of the university and that makes it hard for me to focus and try to understand more about programming. Although it's not my forte, i still focus and try to learn new things so that I can keep up with my studies. In this program, I try to balance my hobbies, studies, and personal needs.

This 2nd year of being a Computer Science student, I realized where I excel, and that is designing. Having a subject called Software Engineering made me know myself, weaknesses, and strengths. I am a Software Designer in our project and it felt more enjoyable rather being a big responsibility, I was able to express my creativity. With that being said, that became my role in this team. I prefer designing rather than programming.
