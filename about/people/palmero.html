About Mary Christelle Palmero

Hi, I’m Mary Christelle Palmero, a second-year Computer Science student at the University of the East Manila. I chose to pursue Computer Science because I am interested in creating systems that can improve efficiency and productivity in society. I enjoy conceptualizing solutions that can help communities, organizations, and even the country operate more effectively on a daily basis.

I consider myself adventurous. I enjoy exploring new ideas and allowing myself to learn from different experiences. I try to approach situations with an open mind, accepting both positive and challenging moments as part of personal growth. I believe that experiences shape perspective and help develop resilience, adaptability, and a deeper understanding of life. Outside academics, I also have a fondness for cats, and I hope to take care of several of them in the future when I have my own home. A single interaction with a cat can bring so much joy and comfort throughout the day.

As of now, I am focusing on learning game development to apply my knowledge of object-oriented programming (OOP). Through this process, I aim to create an outstanding output for our game project. Developing this project allows me to strengthen my problem-solving skills and gain practical experience in system creation. Once the game is completed, I plan to further expand my skills by studying the requirements needed to become a full-stack developer and to understand system architecture more deeply. These areas of knowledge will serve as important foundations for my long-term goal of becoming a system engineer. I aim to contribute to the development of systems that can improve efficiency, support innovation, and provide meaningful benefits to society.
