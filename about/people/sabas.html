About Dave Lance Sabas

Hi, I’m Dave Lance Sabas, I am currently a college student who is studying computer science, and I’m just figuring things out as I go. Curiosity led me to this program because I wanted to learn more about tech, how it works and functions. Over time, curiosity turned into something I actually enjoy learning about. Sometimes it gets frustrating, but I found it really satisfying and fulfilling when something I did finally works. I wouldn’t say I’m an expert at anything yet, because I’m still looking for a specialization to do but I’m always trying to improve. I also joined organizations from the university as I like learning how to work as a team. It is rewarding when I do events for my organizations, being able to function as a team always makes me feel good. As of now, I’m just focused on getting better, even if my progress is slow. I enjoy every step of learning¬, well at least most of the time I do.

Outside of school, I try to keep things balanced and I try to not stay stuck in front of a screen and doom scroll all day. I like doing physical activities like working out at the gym, basketball, or running. I also like doing random stuff depending on my mood, whether it’s watching videos, playing games, or just listen to music. I enjoy hanging out with friends because it helps me relax and forget about most of my problems. There are also days where I just want to do nothing, which is also very relaxing. I think having time to reset is really important, it helps me come back more focused when I need to work again. Overall, I just enjoy doing things while trying to stay responsible.

For the near future, I don’t really have everything planned out yet, but I really want to chase my dreams in basketball or do something related to my college program. I just really want to do something that I enjoy and can keep improving at, and also something sustainable. I’m willing to put in the effort into make my dreams become my reality. I’m also hoping to gain more experience along the way through practices, whether its improving my basketball skills or tech skills. I don’t expect everything to go perfectly, am I’m okay with making mistakes. As long as I keep learning, I feel like I’m on the right track. For now, I’m just trying to stay consistent, avoid distractions, and not give up.
