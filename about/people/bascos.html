About Isaac Bascos

I am a second-year Computer Science student at the University of the East Manila. who is passionate about cybersecurity. I’ve always been interested in how systems work and how they can be protected from threats. This curiosity pushed me to explore technology more deeply and understand how ethical hackers think and operate. Although I sometimes struggle with problem-solving in programming, I treat every challenge as an opportunity to grow and continue improving step by step.

As a second-year Computer Science student, I am currently focused on building my foundation in programming, system design, and web development. I am still in the process of improving my coding and problem-solving skills, especially when dealing with complex tasks, but I continue to practice and learn from my mistakes. I pay close attention to structure and organization when working on systems, as I believe these are important in creating clear and efficient user experiences. Through continuous learning and hands-on practice, I aim to strengthen my technical skills and deepen my understanding of how systems function in real-world applications.

Outside of academics, I spend my time exploring new technologies and improving my programming skills. I enjoy challenging myself, even when things get difficult. In my free time, I play games such as Mobile Legends, Valorant, and Red Dead Redemption 2, and I also spend quality time with my girlfriend, which helps me maintain a healthy balance between school and personal life. I am actively working on improving my time management and reducing procrastination. In the future, I aim to build a career in cybersecurity and contribute to creating secure and reliable digital systems.
