Who We Are

About VerifAI

We are a team of eight passionate individuals united by one goal, helping people navigate a world where AI-generated media is becoming harder to detect than ever before.

Our Mission

Why We Built VerifAI

Artificial intelligence has advanced to a point where fake images look real, synthetic voices sound human, and AI-written articles are nearly indistinguishable from those written by journalists. This is not the future, it is happening right now.

VerifAI was built to fight back through education. We believe that an informed public is the most powerful tool against AI-generated misinformation. Our platform provides clear, accessible guides that teach everyday people, students, parents, workers, and retirees, how to spot the difference between what is real and what is artificially created.

We do not require technical expertise. We just ask that you stay curious, stay critical, and stay real.
